About Everyone's Cultural Life

Our mission is to promote culture and expand accessibility for everyone.

Planning Background

"Everyone's Cultural Life" is a platform designed to increase interest and accessibility to culture. We aim to promote the diversity and beauty of culture to a wider audience. This platform acts as a bridge between people who want to enjoy cultural activities and organizations that host or participate in these activities, enhancing the cultural ecosystem. We provide information on various cultural events, exhibitions, performances, lectures, etc., making it easy for people to participate.

Main Features

"Everyone's Cultural Life" enhances the cultural experience by offering the following key features:

  • Introduction and information about various cultural activities
  • Personalized recommendation system
  • Cultural event and ticket booking features
